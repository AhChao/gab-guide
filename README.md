# Gab Guide - English Small Talk Analyzer

Gab Guide is your personal AI-powered English coach designed to help you master the art of small talk. Simply paste your conversation transcripts, and get instant feedback on your grammar, naturalness, and flow.

[Try it!](https://ahchao.github.io/gab-guide/)
NOTE: remember open in your browser as it would save converstaion history in your browser's local storage.
If you open in a chat app pop up, it probably would not save your conversation history so you would miss it when you try to see it next time.

## ‚ú® Key Features

- **Instant Message Analysis**: Click on any message to get a detailed breakdown of grammar scores and naturalness, along with improved alternatives.
- **Conversation Summaries**: Get a high-level overview of your performance across the entire chat, including clarity and flow evaluation.
- **Vocabulary Boosters**: Discover more natural and advanced phrases tailored to your specific conversation context.
- **Export Reports**: Save your progress as professional PDF or TXT reports to track your improvement over time.
- **Privacy First**: All your conversation history is stored locally in your browser.
